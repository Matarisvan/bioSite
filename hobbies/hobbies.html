Oswaldo's Hobbies

Reading

Oswaldo loves to read. His favorite books span various genres from all over the world. Here we can see a mix of manga, fantasy adventure, fiction, and non-fiction. He likes to read in different languages as well to constantly challenge his knowledge in English, Spanish, and Italian.

Video Games

Oswaldo loves to play video games either by himself or with friends or family members. His favorite video game series include the Zelda series, Street Fighter, F-Zero, Super Smash Bros., etc. He really loves the Nintendo franchise since that is what he and I mostly played throughout our growing years. In this picture we can see him competing at the EVO Championship Series. EVO is one of the biggest video game tournaments in the world and due to COVID restrictions, Oswaldo had to opportunity to play from home.

Working Out

The one thing I believe that Oswaldo is obsessed with the most in this world is going to the gym and working out. It does not matter what is going on around him or if there is anything planned. This man will find the time to go to the gym and workout for a few hours. I cannot stress how much he enjoys his time at the gym. There have been various occasions where my family is late to an event because they had to wait for Oswaldo to return from his workout.
